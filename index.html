About Me

My Programing Certificate

I was awarded a completion certificate with a 9.0 overall score for my capstone project.

This academic pursuit enhanced my programming, Python, SQL database management, query writing, logical, and analytical skills.

Automation Frameworks

Upon completing the Python foundation module, I developed an interest in automation frameworks, specifically Robot Framework and Karate Framework(the team's main framework). Concurrently, I was afforded the opportunity to apply my automation knowledge to a work project, where I was responsible for developing test scripts for the internal administration management for a restaurant. Due to the simultaneous pursuit of learning and project work, I obtained the course completion certificate in July.
